## ADDED Requirements
### Requirement: Jeung6kei2 AI Opponent
The system SHALL support an AI opponent for Jeung6kei2 with legal move generation and timely responses.

#### Scenario: Play vs AI
- **WHEN** the user starts Jeung6kei2 in Human vs AI mode
- **THEN** the AI side makes a legal move automatically after the human move within 3 seconds under normal device conditions
- **AND** Undo, Save, Load, and Reset continue to function

#### Scenario: AI Side Selection
- **WHEN** the user selects AI mode before starting Jeung6kei2
- **THEN** they MAY choose whether AI plays Red or Black
- **AND** the game starts with turns set accordingly
